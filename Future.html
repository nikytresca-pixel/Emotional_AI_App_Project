Challenges and Considerations

While AI brings tremendous opportunities to HCI, it also presents important challenges. Privacy concerns, algorithmic bias, transparency, and the need for human oversight are critical considerations as we design AI-powered interfaces.

The future of HCI must balance the power of AI with ethical design principles that prioritize user agency, fairness, and trust. Successful AI-powered interfaces will augment human capabilities rather than replace human decision-making.
