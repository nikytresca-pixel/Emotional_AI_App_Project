HUMAN COMPUTER INTERACTION:
Why is the User So Important?

The Central Role of the User

Human-computer interaction (HCI) refers to a user-centered approach of design and development that optimizes synergy between human and computer. We can compare HCI to a continuous cycle, as each human action is followed by a response of the computer, and after that we act again.


Rather than designing technology for technology's sake, HCI prioritizes the needs, goals, and limitations of the user. This involves extensive research into human factors, cognitive psychology, social sciences, and design principles.

Responsive Design: Ensuring Consistent User Experience

Responsive design is an approach to web design which aims at allowing desktop pages to be viewed in response to the size of the screen, in order to facilitate usability, navigation and information seeking.


Responsive design has significant benefits, as you may:

  • Reach a larger audience across all devices;
  • Save time and development efforts with a single codebase;
  • Ensure design consistency across devices and platforms;
  • Improve SEO and search engine rankings;
  • Reduce maintenance costs and complexity.

The Use of Graphical and Interaction Elements

In Human–Computer Interaction, responsive design is not only about screen size, but about adapting the interface to users, contexts, and devices. Graphical and interaction elements play a central role in this adaptation.


Here are some universal interaction elements with their meanings:

 Happy Emotion: It expresses genuine appreciation in a constructive way, delivering positive and approving feedback.
Sad Emotion: More than just an icon, it's a visual hug, representing empathy, support, and emotional care. Across all devices, it works to communicate reassurance and put human values first. This simple expression of care is key to building trust and a genuine emotional connection.
Angry Emotion: This icon is designed for moments of frustration. It represents a deep breath and a step toward calmness. Across all devices, it gently communicates emotional feedback, allowing users to recognize and settle their feelings with just a glance.
Bored Emotion: This heart icon is a spark of joy, designed to lift spirits and re-engage. Across all devices, it delivers an instant boost of friendly energy, helping to turn a moment of boredom into one of connection and appreciation.
